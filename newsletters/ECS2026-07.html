Studio Notes

Featured: The Forged Iron Stand

This iron stand was perfect for showcasing hand-thrown ceramics. The design had to be both functional and beautiful, taking into account the form and the materials used. Scrolls and curves were incorporated to add visual interest and to complement the organic shapes of the pottery.

The final piece demonstrates the successful combination of traditional techniques with modern design sensibilities.
