Vision

"My creative practice is a continuous act of exploration and discovery—a framework for navigating both the raw beauty of the natural world and the complex social structures that define the human experience. Through tactile mediums like ceramics, stone, and steel, alongside precise visual storytelling in photography, I seek to cultivate spaces for deep self-reflection, collective healing, and the reinforcement of community bonds in an increasingly fragmented world."
