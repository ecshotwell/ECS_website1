Social Advocacy

As an artist, my creative output is inseparable from civic responsibility. My work is evolving to demonstrate a conscious protest against those who promote mistrust and division.

Countering Injustice

Art that points out societal issues can challenge authorities to address them. My bronze casting of "Resistance over Tyranny" is an example of this. A fragile but brave soul can muster the strength to stand against oppression and injustice.

Community Engagement

An artist that produces work that brings people together and fosters community bonds is a powerful force for healing.

From the Studio

The Potter's Shed

The steady hum of a spinning wheel and the roar of a lit forge carry their own quiet rhythm. Through this blog, I want to unpack that studio energy and share honest reflections on art, life, and the creative process. It makes me wonder: what occupies your mind when you are fully immersed in creating?
