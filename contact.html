Eric Shotwell, Artist Blacksmith

Common Interests

For me, making art is all about exploring the world. I love finding unique shapes in our local landscapes. Then, I use my hands to turn those ideas into real art. I want my physical work to show these values. I use the hot fire of the forge and the steady rhythm of the pottery wheel. These tools help start friendly conversations and bring neighbors closer together.

Partnering with an Artist

When you buy a piece of art, you choose to partner with the artist. This partnership works best when we share a sense of community. It also requires a commitment to hard work. Making art is a wonderful way to bring creative ideas to life. It gives you something real to experience every single day.

What is Important to You?

Choosing to collect art is a big decision. It takes much time and thought to bring a vision to life. You might want a beautiful sculpture for your home. Or, you might want to step up to the anvil to learn a traditional craft yourself. Either way, you are choosing to appreciate hard work and dedication.
