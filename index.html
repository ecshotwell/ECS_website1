Creativity and Vision

Our imagination creates opportunities for beautiful art. Some are called to enhance their surroundings through the artistry of ornamental blacksmithing. Others look for functional and sculptural work in stone and ceramics. I hope we have the opportunity to collaborate and create something truly special together.

Inspiration from Eastern Europe

Let's begin by exploring the rich artistic heritage of Eastern Europe. Much of my work is influenced by European architecture. Blacksmiths and stone masons have created a legacy of intricate designs and innovative techniques that define the region's artistic tradition.
