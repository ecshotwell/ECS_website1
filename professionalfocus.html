Eric Shotwell, Artist Blacksmith

Professional Focus

Eric Shotwell's professional and creative focus is centered on artistic exploration and social justice advocacy. His work is characterized by the following areas: Fine Arts and Crafts: He creates tangible artwork specializing in ceramics (including Tenmoku, wood-fired, and earth tones) as well as steel and stone art. Photography: Through his "Beauty Around Us" initiative, he focuses on capturing natural imagery and unique treasures during walks in Central Indiana. Social and Philosophical Reflection: As highlighted on his website subtitle, "Exploration And Discovery," he promotes community healing, deep self-reflection, and understanding social bonds to counter fear tactics and social divisions.
