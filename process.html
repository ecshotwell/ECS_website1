The Forged Tool

Every dynamic tool begins its life as a static, rusty lump of iron. Through precise filing, grinding, polishing, and balancing, it is transformed into a highly responsive extension of the hand. This restoration process isn't just about restoring functionality; it's a deep study in patience and a reflection on how we reshape old, forgotten things into purposeful instruments.
